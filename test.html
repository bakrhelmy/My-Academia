Smart Parking System

Automated Parking Management

Our Smart Parking system optimizes parking space utilization in academic institutions through real-time monitoring and automated status updates.

Components:

IR Sensors Green LEDs Red LEDs ESP32

How It Works:

IR sensors detect the presence of a car in a parking spot. When a car parks, the sensor triggers a signal sent via the ESP32 to Firebase, updating the mobile app to show the spot as occupied (red LED on). If the spot is empty, the green LED lights up, and the app reflects this status.

This system optimizes parking management by providing real-time availability data, reducing congestion and saving time for students and staff.

Smart Garden System

Automated Plant Care

The Smart Garden system maintains optimal growing conditions through automated irrigation and climate control.

Components:

Soil Moisture Sensor Water Pump DHT11 Sensor 12V Fan ESP32

How It Works:

The soil moisture sensor monitors the ground's water level. If the soil becomes too dry, it signals the ESP32 to activate the water pump, irrigating the soil until the sensor indicates sufficient moisture.

The DHT11 sensor tracks temperature and humidity, which is useful for adapting to different plants' needs. If the temperature rises too high, the ESP32 turns on the 12V fan to cool the area until conditions stabilize.

Data from these sensors is sent to Firebase, allowing remote monitoring via the app. This creates an automated, self-sustaining garden tailored to environmental conditions.

Smart Lecture Hall

Intelligent Classroom Management

The Smart Hall integrates attendance tracking, environmental control, and safety features for a seamless educational experience.

Components:

ESP32-CAM Flame Sensor Smoke Sensor Buzzer IR Sensor LED Strip 12V Fan

How It Works:

Attendance: The ESP32-CAM handles face detection for attendance, storing data in a spreadsheet and sending notifications.

Environmental Control: When the camera identifies a professor entering, it automatically triggers a sequence turning on lights, adjusting curtains, and activating the projector for lecture slides.

Safety: Flame and smoke sensors monitor for potential hazards. The buzzer alarms and notifications are sent when danger is detected.

Comfort: The LED strip and 12V fan adjust lighting and temperature based on occupancy and conditions, enhancing the hall's environment.
