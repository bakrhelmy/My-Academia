App Features

Comprehensive campus management in one powerful application

Home Screen

The central hub providing quick access to all campus features including buildings, garage, garden, and attendance tracking.

Hall Control

Select buildings and halls, then control lights, fans, and projectors with live streaming capabilities for each hall.

Parking Management

Real-time monitoring of parking spaces with infrared sensors showing available (green) and occupied (red) spots.

Garden Automation

Monitor temperature and humidity with automatic fan control. Soil moisture sensors trigger water pumps when needed.

App Screens

Explore our intuitive user interface

Home Screen

Home Screen

It serves as the starting point from which users can access the application's main features, content, and navigation options, such as going to the buildings page, the garage page, the garden page, or the attendance page.

Login & Signup

Login & Signup Screens

The user first selects either sign up or login to verify the user's identity. The login screen allows users to access their existing accounts, while the sign up screen allows new users to create accounts to use the app.

Hall Control

Hall Screens

Here, we start by choosing the building from building A, building B, etc, and then we choose the hall we are in from Hall A, Hall B, etc. Also, after choosing the hall, I can control everything there from lights, fans, or projectors, and I can open a live stream for the entire hall.

Parking Management

Parking Screens

On the parking screen, we display empty or full spaces. We have an infrared sensor to detect any car present. As long as the space is empty, the green light is on, and if a car has parked, the red light is on.

Garden Automation

Garden Screens

On the garden screen, we display the temperature and humidity, and if the temperature is high, we turn on the fan, and we can control it. There is also a soil moisture sensor to measure the water in the soil. If the soil has good water content, we do not turn on the water pump, and if the soil is dry, we make the water pump work automatically.
