Smart Garden System

Automated garden maintenance with intelligent environmental control

Real-Time Monitoring

Our app displays current temperature and humidity levels in your garden. When temperatures rise above optimal levels, the system automatically activates cooling fans to maintain the perfect environment for your plants.

Smart Irrigation

The soil moisture sensor continuously measures water content in the soil. When moisture levels drop below the threshold, the water pump automatically activates to irrigate your garden, then turns off when optimal moisture is reached.

Smart Parking System

Real-time parking space detection and management

Space Availability

The app shows real-time parking space status. Each space is clearly marked with its identifier and color-coded to show availability at a glance.

Infrared Detection

Infrared sensors detect vehicle presence in each parking space. When a space is empty, the indicator shows green. When occupied, it turns red.
