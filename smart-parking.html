Smart Hall Components

The Smart Hall system integrates multiple IoT devices to create an automated, intelligent classroom environment with attendance tracking, environmental control, and safety monitoring.

Environmental Sensors
Includes: DHT11 (Temp/Humidity), MQ-2 (Smoke), KY-026 (Flame)

Integration

  • Continuous environmental monitoring
  • Triggers alarms and notifications
  • Data logged to Firebase for analytics
  • Fan control based on temperature
