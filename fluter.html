App Features

Comprehensive campus management in one powerful application

Home Screen

The central hub providing quick access to all campus features including buildings, garage, garden, and attendance tracking.

Hall Control

Select buildings and halls, then control lights, fans, and projectors with live streaming capabilities for each hall.

Parking Management

Real-time monitoring of parking spaces with infrared sensors showing available (green) and occupied (red) spots.

Garden Automation

Monitor temperature and humidity with automatic fan control. Soil moisture sensors trigger water pumps when needed.

Home Screen

Your central hub for navigating the smart system

Main Navigation Hub

The Home Screen acts as the main entry point to the application, offering users a seamless interface to explore and access all core features.

Quick Access to Key Sections

From the Home Screen, users can quickly navigate to various sections, including the Buildings Page, Garage (Parking) Page, Garden Page, and the Attendance Page—making control and monitoring intuitive and efficient.

Attendance Control

Efficient tracking and management of attendance

Real-time Tracking

Monitor attendance in real-time with our intuitive interface that shows current status of students and faculty across all halls and buildings.

Automated Reports

Generate detailed attendance reports automatically with our system that tracks and records all attendance data for analysis and record-keeping.

Smart Garden System

Automated garden maintenance with intelligent environmental control

Real-Time Climate Monitoring

Monitor your garden's temperature and humidity in real time. When the temperature exceeds the ideal range, the cooling fan automatically turns on to protect your plants and maintain a stable environment.

Intelligent Soil Irrigation

The soil moisture sensor constantly checks the water level in the soil. If it detects dryness, the water pump is automatically triggered to irrigate the garden. Once the soil reaches the required moisture level, the pump shuts off—ensuring water efficiency and healthy plant growth.

Smart Parking System

Real-time parking space monitoring with visual indicators

Live Space Detection

Our system uses infrared sensors to detect the presence of vehicles in real time. Each parking slot is monitored individually to determine whether it is occupied or vacant.

LED Status Indicators

When a parking spot is empty, a green LED light is turned on. Once a car parks in the spot, the system switches the light to red, providing a clear and immediate visual indicator for drivers and monitoring systems.
